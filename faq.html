Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can’t afford solar?

We have zero-down options and flexible financing. Most customers pay less monthly than they do with Duke.

How does net metering work in Greensboro?

Your meter runs backward when you produce extra electricity, giving you bill credits.

Does Duke still send me a bill?

You'll still get a bill, but most customers see dramatic reductions right away.

What happens if I sell my home?

Solar increases your home's value. You can transfer your system or roll it into the home sale.
